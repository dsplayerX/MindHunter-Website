"There is no friend
as loyal as a book."

- Ernest Hemingway

In this quote Ernest Hemingway speaks to the emotional attachment one can have towards a book. The way in which a book can make us feel, the way in which it can open the door to new worlds and ways of seeing. A book is different depending on who is reading it and therefore perhaps we have unspoken attachments to our books. Our books are always there for us; they are our most loyal friends.
