About Us


What are the key benifits of using Mindhunter Bookstore?

Customer is the number one priority in everything we do. We do our best to serve the customer to the best of our ability. Our service is a hassle free way to get the books you want, whenever you want. Thanks to our huge catalogue and speed dilivery service partners, we guarentee you get your order in less time than the mentioned time whichever the shipping method you use. Have fun, browse books, and collect and share knowledge!

Delivery


What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges are dependent on the delivery requirements. Costs are dependent on your location and products on your order. You can see the shipping fees on the checkout process before the payment is made.


Can I track my order?

We will send you the tracking code of the shipment when the parcel has been sent.


What is the usual delivery time?

Delivery time will be 2-5 working days for local shipping. Delivery time will vary depending on the courier for international delivery.


Changing the delivery address?

You can change the delivery address during the checkout process. If you need to change delivery address after the order has been placed, please contact us at contact.us@mindhunter.com as soon as possible and we'll change it for you.


What does it mean that the order status is "Closed?"

If your order status is Closed, the order has been shipped and should arrive within1 to 2 weeks.


I waited so long for this order and now items are cancelled. Will I get a refund?

Cancelled items were not charged, so refunds are not pending for cancelled items.


What are the possible delivery methods for my order?

On all delivery orders, customers may choose from USPS Media Mail, USPS Priority Mail and UPS GROUND.


Can I return a product?

Yes, you can. Within 7 days of the delivery date, if you want to return a product you bought, please contact contact.us@mindhinter.com.



Discounts


Do you have seasonal discounts?

Yes, we do. We have seasonal discounts around new year's time and our biggest discounts and offer in Christmas month. Starting 1st December till the 31st, all our books and some stationary items are 15% off and selected books and genres get even bigger discounts up to 55%.


Do you offer a special discount for students?

Yes, and no! We have discounts up to 20% for selected academic books which is not just for students, but for everybody. Feel free to browse the website and get the book you want at the lowest price possible.



Payments


Can I cancel my order after paying?

If you want to cancel your order, please do so as soon as possible. If we have already processed your order, you need to contact us and return the product. Please contact contact.us@mindhinter.com


How do I pay for my order?

You can pay with direct bank transfer or paypal to our account number: 23944121933. We will match it with your order and send you your ordered items as soon as possible.


What should I do if the payment is not accepted?

Please try again in a little while. If the payment is still not accepted, please verify your account balance. If everything is as it should, but you still can't make the payment, please contact contact.us@mindhinter.com notify us about the problem.
